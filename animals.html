Honey Badger

Honey Badgers will eat anything, They’re omnivores who will go after everything even mammals and birds.

Cheetah

Cheetahs are the only big cats that can’t roar, and Mortality rates for cheetah cubs in Africa run as high as 95%.

White Rhinoceros

Both black and white rhinoceroses are actually gray. They are different in lipshape. The white rhinoceros relative has a squared lip.

African lions

African lions are revered the world over, but their population has shrunk in half over the past 25 years.

American Bison

Bison, symbolic animals of the Great Plains, are often mistakenly called buffaloes, they are the heaviest land animals in North America.

Plains Zebra

No animal has a more distinctive coat than the zebra. Each animal's stripes are as unique as fingerprints—no two are exactly alike.

Leopard

Leopards are graceful and powerful big cats. They live in sub-Saharan Africa, northeast Africa, Central Asia, India, and China.
